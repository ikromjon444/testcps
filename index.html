What is CPS Test?

The cpstest aka click test is more likely a timepass game where you can set the highest clicks per second score and break others' records too. It allows you to test your finger speed on the mouse to check how speedily you can click on the mouse button.

How fast can you click?

Most players at the click speed test managed to click between 5-10 clicks per second. To become the faster in clicks you need to click between 5 to 10 clicks per second. CPS Test defines some ranks upon your clicking speed. But if you want to avoid becoming a turtle then you must click above 5 clicks per second.

How do you calculate Clicks Per Second?

Click per second is nothing but calculating the frequency of your clicks. You can calculate your CPS by Dividing your Total Number of Clicks by the time you took for clicking.

CPS = Total Number of Clicks / Total Time in Seconds

What is CPS in Minecraft?

In Minecraft PvP, clicking speed is vital. Minecraft itself can only register (Roughly) 2 clicks per second from players, but higher CPS gives you a "Reach" advantage during sprint breaking fights.

  • 4-7 CPS: Moderately slow, okay to PvP.
  • 8-13 CPS: Moderately high, complicated PvP mechanics.
  • 14+ CPS: Extremely fast, massive advantage.

What is Kohi Click Test?

The Kohi Click Test is a test used to measure a minecraft player's clicking speed. It is specifically popular in the Minecraft community and is often used on the Minecraft server called Kohi.
